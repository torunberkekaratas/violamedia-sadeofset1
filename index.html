About · Our values

We take our strength from our values.

Who we are

Sade Ofset provides printing services to companies across a wide range of industries, and is capable of performing its production within its own facilities — thanks to machinery capable of offset, flexographic and letterpress printing.

For half a century we have built our reputation on consistency: tight registration, faithful colour and packaging that performs on the shelf and on the line.

How we work

We have been working for 50 years with the same dedication; continuing the honest, just and human-focused business model taught to us by our founder.

Our employees work and serve as a family — and that same care carries through to every job we deliver for our customers.
